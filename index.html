About Us

Established in 2022, Averytech is a rising star in the Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) industry. Our mission is to set new standards in this field by delivering high-quality, high-precision and on-time delivery solutions, that meet and exceed our customer's diverse needs and requirements.

Our Vision

We are driven by a vision to become a renowned name in the EMS industry, recognized for our unwavering commitment to excellence. We aspire to leave an indelible mark in various key verticals, including industry, automobile, defense, medical, and aerospace. Our journey towards these verticals is guided by our dedication to innovation and our unrelenting pursuit of perfection.
